Ingredients

  • chicken thighs
  • ginger
  • sesame oil
  • soy sauce

Steps

  1. Toss the chicken into instant pot
  2. chop up some ginger and toss it in too
  3. drizzle sesame oil over chicken and ginger
  4. dump soy sauce on top
  5. set instant pot to cook for 45 minutes
  6. enjoy!
