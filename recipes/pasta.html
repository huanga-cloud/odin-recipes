Ingredients

  • dry pasta (rotini, chickpea!)
  • pasta sauce of choice
  • water
  • olive oil
  • chicken sausage
  • spinach
  • mushrooms
  • parmesan

Instructions

  1. bring water to boil
  2. while waiting for water to boil, chop up sausage and brown it in olive oil
  3. water should be done, so dump in the pasta
  4. set timer for 8 minutes, chop up mushrooms and dump them in along with the spinach
  5. sausage should be done, so turn off the fire
  6. wait for pasta to finish (8 minutes)
  7. after it's done, drain pasta, and toss in sausage
  8. toss on some parmesan cheese + sauce, mix
  9. serve!
